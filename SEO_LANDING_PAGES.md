## SEO Benefits

### 1. **Keyword Targeting**
Each page targets 5-10 high-intent, long-tail keywords with strong search volume in Canada:
- "how to switch to credit union Canada"
- "start a housing co-op Canada"
- "worker buyout Canada"
- "Citizens Wealth Fund explained"

### 2. **On-Page SEO**
- **Proper H1 tags** with primary keywords
- **H2/H3 hierarchy** for content structure
- **Meta titles** optimized for CTR (under 60 chars)
- **Meta descriptions** with compelling CTAs (under 160 chars)
- **Keyword density** naturally distributed throughout content

### 3. **Rich Snippets (Schema.org)**
Each page includes structured data for:
- **HowTo** schema with step-by-step instructions
- **Estimated time** (totalTime property)
- **Position-based steps** for numbered lists

This enables Google to display:
- Step-by-step guides directly in search results
- Featured snippets with time estimates
- Rich cards with action buttons

### 4. **FAQ Sections**
Each page includes 6 FAQs targeting long-tail queries:
- "Are credit unions safe Canada?" → Direct answer in FAQ
- "Can I sell my CLT home?" → Ranking for informational intent
- "How much do union dues cost?" → Capturing objection-based searches

### 5. **Internal Linking**
- **Homepage "Choose Your Track" section** links to all 4 pages
- **Cross-links between tracks** in "Explore Other Tracks" footer
- **Resource links** connect to existing toolkits (/resources/*)
- **CTA links** to /join with anchor tags (#pledge, #union, #founders)

### 6. **User Experience**
- **Stats grids** with compelling numbers (social proof)
- **Comparison tables** (old system vs. new solution)
- **Testimonials** with names and locations (authenticity)
- **Step-by-step guides** with visual hierarchy
- **Email capture CTAs** for conversion

### 7. **Mobile Optimization**
- Responsive grid layouts (md:grid-cols-2, lg:grid-cols-3)
- Touch-friendly cards with hover states
- Readable font sizes (text-xl, text-lg)
- Collapsible FAQ accordions (Card components)
